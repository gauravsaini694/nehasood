About Us

Know Your Doctor
  • Dr. Neha Sood is a Ear, Nose & Throat Surgeon currently working as a Associate Director at BLK-Max Superspeciality Hospital, Delhi
  • Dr. Neha Sood specializes in the treatment of hearing loss and leads the program on Cochlear Implants at BLK Max Super-Specialiaty Hospital
  • Her fields of interest and expertise include Endoscopic sinus and skull base surgery
Experience
  • Dr Sood has more than 18 years of experience in treating all ear nose throat disorders.
  • Presenlty working as a Associate Director in ENT & Cochlear Implant Department at BLK-Max Super Speciality Hospital, New Delhi
  • She has worked as ENT surgeon at president estate clinic Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi
  • Currently part of Endoscopic Skull Base Surgery Team at BLK-Max Super Speciality Hospital
  • Formerly worked as a consultant at Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, Primus Hospital, Sita Ram Bhartia Hospital
